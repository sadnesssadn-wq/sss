The story behind our name

Why are you called Millenial host? Isn’t it spelt Millennial?

There are a myriad of stories and theories as to why we called ourselves Millenial. We’ll share them all, and you can decide for yourself which YOU think is true.

  1. The domain Millennial.Host was already taken, and we just value the .host extension more than having a correctly spelled name
  2. Clive being a native English speaker, and Jay being a native Spanish speaker, we decided to go for a hybrid spelling of Millennial and Milenial. Makes sense right? Still better than being called Clay.Host… or Jive.Host. It’s only logical right?
  3. We thought Millenial looks better. After all, Google spelled their name wrong. It’s actually spelled googol!
  4. Spelling the name wrong creates buzz. Just look. You’re here, reading an entire page about why we called ourselves MILLENIAL. C’mon! That’s publicity you can’t buy.
  5. Because we're millennials (I mean millenials) and we don’t care about correct spelling. What self respecting millenial (see what I did there?) would? That’s why they invented autocorrect.
  6. Because Jay can’t spell and Clive can’t spell check, but it was too late to change because we already registered the domain.
But wait, aren’t you called MLNL.HOST?

Why yes, yes we are! That’s because Jay can’t spell and Clive cant spell check! And the next best thing to having a correctly spelled name is a name where we remove ALL the vowels. Definitely makes our name seem intentional and not a mistake. Not at all. Say our name now. MLNLl…. Rolls right off the tongue doesn’t it.
